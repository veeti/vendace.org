Quick screenshot crop & upload.

Vendace lets you quickly snap a screenshot, crop it and upload it to imgur in just a few clicks. The resulting URL is instantly copied to your clipboard ready for sharing.

Want to redact something or just make silly doodles on it first? Set an external editor from the settings and launch it with a single click. Edit, save and close to return back to Vendace.

And of course, saving to disk is also supported.

Download & Install

The current version is 0.1.2. Packages are available for Arch Linux (development package). Source packages.
